Chicken Alfredo

Prep and Cook Time: 30 minutes

Serves 4


This version of chicken alfredo is the classic American version. Brought to the United States in the early 1900's by Italian immigrants.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. (450g) boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lbs (453g) tagliatelle
  • 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • Cooking spray
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 c (22g) grated Parmigiano Reggiano, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 c (22g) grated Pecorino Romano
  • 1/2 cup (118ml) skim milk
  • 2 Tbls. (28g) unsalted butter
  • 1 large zucchini, diced into 1/2" pieces
