Pancakes

Prep and Cook Time: 25 minutes

Serves 4


Traditional American pancakes made with creamy buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. (240ml) buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 c. (300g) AP flour
  • 1/2 c. (120ml) whole milk
  • 1/4 c. (62g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp. (4g) fine sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp. (1g) baking soda
  • 1 Tbls. (12g) baking powder
  • 2 Tbls. (21g) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp (4g) vanilla extract, optional
  • Butter to finish
  • Maple syrup to finish

Instructions

  1. Into a large bowl, add AP flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Whisk together until fully combined.
  2. Then, to the mixing bowl, add buttermilk, whole milk, egg, egg yolk, and vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Whisk until homogeneous, being careful not to overmix.
  3. Preheat a griddle, or non-stick skillet, over medium heat. Once to temerature, lightly spray with cooking spray.
  4. Once hot, spoon 1/4" dollops of the pancake batter. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown on the bottom.
  5. Flip ad cook for an additonal 2-3 minutes. Repeat with the rest of the batter.
  6. Top with butter and drizzle with maple syrup to serve.
